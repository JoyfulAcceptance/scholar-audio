# Challenges, Decisions, and Learning

## Writing prompt

Choose the few difficulties that reveal the most about the product. Describe the decision made, not every detour taken.

## Ideas to consider

- PDF extraction preserves words more easily than listening structure
- Natural voice quality does not guarantee good scholarly prosody
- Newer Premium voices may interpret legacy `say` pacing commands differently
- A chaptered audiobook is more useful than a pile of disconnected tracks
- Privacy pushed the project toward local tools and away from cloud TTS
- The interface became simpler as the product decision became clearer: audio only, or PDF plus audio
- Listening tests were more informative than theoretical polishing
- Submission discipline required moving advanced pronunciation and grammatical-pause ideas out of the MVP
